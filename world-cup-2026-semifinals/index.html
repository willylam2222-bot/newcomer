The semifinals: where they are, how to get in, and where to watch

The semifinals are the last big search spike before the final — cheaper than July 19, just as intense, and the moment resale inventory swings wildly as fans of eliminated teams sell out.

By the Newcomer editorial team · Last verified July 2026 · 4 min read

By the semifinal stage the field is down to four and every match is a potential classic. If you missed the group and quarterfinal windows, the semis are your best-value shot at a live World Cup match — and the run-up to the final on the 19th.

When and where

  • The two semifinals sit in the days before the July 19 final, in two of the tournament's marquee host venues. Confirm the exact date, venue and kickoff for your match on FIFA's official schedule — knockout venues are only fixed once the bracket resolves.
  • Plan travel around the venue city, not the stadium: World Cup host stadiums sit outside downtowns, so factor transit and go early (see our MetLife matchday guide for the New Jersey venue).

Getting a semifinal ticket now

  • FIFA's official resale platform is the only guaranteed-authentic fan-to-fan channel. Inventory surges the morning after quarterfinal eliminations — fans of knocked-out teams dump seats. Check right after each result.
  • Official hospitality packages remain available late for four figures — the reliable last door if resale is dry.
  • Semifinal seats run cheaper than the final but dynamic pricing still bites — expect several hundred dollars and up. Anything dramatically below the official market is a scam. If payment leaves FIFA's system, assume the ticket doesn't exist.

Can't get in? Watch it right

  • The FIFA Fan Festivals in every host city screen both semifinals free on giant screens — the same electric crowd as the final, no ticket needed.
  • Streaming splits by country — use your national official broadcaster or its app, never a final-week "free stream" link. (Full breakdown in our where-to-watch guide.)
Best-value play

A semifinal is 90% of the final's atmosphere at a fraction of the price and scarcity. Watch the quarterfinal results, refresh official resale the next morning, and pounce.
